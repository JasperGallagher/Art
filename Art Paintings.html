Egyptian Falcon

In this picture you can see that a falcon is praising some kind of royal character. During the Egyptian time falcons represented freedom, light, superiority spirit, and aspiration.

Egyptian Cat Slaying a Snake

Cats during ancient Egyptian times were praised because they had the ability to kill snakes and were supposedly able to control vermin, an insect that is believed to be dangerous to crops, farm animals and game. They also became a symbol of grace and poise.
