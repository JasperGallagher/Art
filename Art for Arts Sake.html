Our Story

For multiple years we've been collecting and exploring Egyptian Art from 3000 BC till about the third century. So that the poeple at home can learn, see, and love all the art they have made. Recently we have even found remnants of an egyptian boat in our exhibit. We also currently have some of our top archaeologists searching for new undiscovered art.

Our Exhibits

Ancient Woodworking

In this exhibit you can see all of the wonderful woodworking the ancient Egyptians used to make. Ranging from Art to carvings we have it all in this exhibit.

The Boat Exhibit

In this Exhibit you can see real remnants from the boats the Egyptians used to use to sail up and down the Nile River as well as a full sized replica of what we think they used to have.

The Pharaoh's Exhibit

Come in to this exhibit and learn all of about the Egyptian Pharoahs. Here you can learn about Cleopatra and of course the famous King Tutankhamun. There even is soem statues that the Egyptians used to make for their Pharoahs.

Mummie's Exhibit

Come here and you'll see the ways the Egyptians used to wrap their important people. We even have a mummie that some of our archaeologists had found a few years ago. We also have a mummie wrapping class for you kids so you can learn to wrap a mummie.
